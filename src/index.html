About Us

The South End

SENCo Board members serve the community by working together with the neighborhood to accomplish our goals within the South End boundaries. Responsibilities and Duties: Advocate for all South End residents. Participate regularly in Board meetings. Review and vote on issues that work toward our goals and plans to support our community. Serve actively on one or more committees. Work towards building relationships that strengthen our community and SENCo. Participate in community activities. Respond to community concerns & seek community input for decisions. Encourage the growth and development of other community groups. Appropriately use City and local government agency information & resources to help address community issues. Support services that strengthen youth, family, and community life in the South End.

One of the great ways to get involved with the South End Neighborhood Council is to consider joining one of our committees.
